Quality Identifier

The Quality Identifier solution leverages advanced sensor technologies, such as Laser-Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS), to perform real-time, online identification of different fertilizer types and detect foreign bodies. This automated system ensures precise classification and monitoring of material quality throughout the production and handling processes.

Impact/ Value Creation

  • By accurately identifying and classifying different fertilizer types, the system ensures consistent product quality and adherence to standards.
  • The ability to detect foreign bodies reduces the risk of contamination and potential hazards, ensuring a safer working environment.
  • Automation of the quality identification process minimizes manual intervention, streamlining operations and reducing the likelihood of human error.
  • By preventing contamination and ensuring consistent quality, the solution helps in reducing waste and optimizing resource utilization, leading to cost savings.
  • Provides continuous, real-time data on material quality, allowing for immediate corrective actions and better decision-making in the production process.
