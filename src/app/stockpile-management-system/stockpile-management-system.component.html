Stockpile Management System

Optimizes material tracking, material handling and quality management for the fertilizer stockpiles. Achieve a fully automated operations of stockyard providing the possibility to have a manual mode to high level automation of complex stacking and reclaiming. Provide advanced automation of stacking and reclaiming operations for bulk material stockyards using centralized 3D models and advanced automation algorithms. Real-time 3D visualization for accurate monitoring of stockyards, stockpiles, and machines. Additionally, SMS allows tracking of material, quality, and age throughout the stockyard.
