Route Management System

Automated route management and optimization, from the storage area to the port. The system calculates the most efficient route for transporting fertilizer from selected warehouses to a ship vessel, considering factors like distance, traffic conditions, time constraints, and energy consumption.

Features

Efficient Routing

Calculates the most efficient route for transporting fertilizer, optimizing for distance, traffic, time, and energy consumption.

Intermediate Locations

Allows defining intermediate locations between the warehouse and the vessel for an optimized route.

Material Compatibility

Prevents cross-contamination by considering material compatibility in route planning.

Impact/Value Creation

  • Increased Efficiency
  • Optimal Resource Utilization (including energy)
  • Reduced Operational Costs
  • Improved Route Accuracy and Management
