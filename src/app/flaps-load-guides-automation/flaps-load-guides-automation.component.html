Flaps and Load Guides Automation

Automatic control of flaps ("volets") and load guides ("guide-charges") positions, ensuring optimal material flow on conveyor belts from stock to port.

Features

Proximity Sensors

Detects the presence or absence of materials on conveyor belts to activate or deactivate flaps and load guides.

Position Sensors

Provides real-time feedback on the positions of flaps and load guides for precise material loading.

Actuators

Controls the movement of flaps and load guides, allowing automated adjustments based on material flow.

Impact/Value Creation

  • Reducing stops caused by manual operations and defects
  • Reducing leaks, which minimizes cleaning and recycling costs
  • Decreasing the cost of circuit preparation and reducing reaction time
