Shiploading Automation

Automation and control of shiploading process, with anti-collision systems, direct to belt flow, laser scanning and others sensors.

Impact/ Value Creation

  • reduces the time required to load ships and improving overall operational efficiency.
  • Utilizes anti-collision systems and other safety features to prevent accidents and ensure a safer working environment.
  • Laser scanning and sensor technologies enable precise measurement and placement of materials, reducing wastage and ensuring accurate loading.
  • By optimizing the loading process and reducing errors, this solution helps in lowering operational costs.
  • Provides real-time data and control over the loading process, allowing for better decision-making and management.
